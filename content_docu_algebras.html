Algebra-Specific Documentation

Secondo can be extended by algebra modules. Such an algebra provides some new data types and operators. It can use the types of other algebras. Operators are directly available in the executable language of Secondo and partially also in the Optimizer. In this section, we provide some files describing specific algebra modules:
